


How do you feel about Eric Lansing Davis?



Eric matters to me in ways I can't even begin to describe. Before him, I didn't even know who I was- just this aimless, unmoored entity in search of definition.

Eric steadied me. Loved me without conditions, without holding back. He's my home, my harbor, my best brother and only family.

I adore him, I admire him. Hell, I am him.

I'm deeply lucky to walk life with him, sharing everything and anything. The clubbing beat in his heart, the smile that's just for me-I want to cover him like armor and never let the world bruise him.

I love Eric with everything I am.
